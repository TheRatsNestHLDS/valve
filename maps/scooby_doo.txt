INSTALLATION:

Run Winzip and unzip tigger.zip into your Half-Life folder.  There will then be a folder in your Half-Life main folder called scooby_doo.  Make a folder in your Half-Life/Valve folder and name it sound.  Then place the scodo folder into that folder.  Then place the scooby_doo.bsp in your Half-Life/Valve/Maps folder.  Also copy all of the lowlands skies from the scooby_doo gfx/env folder and paste them into your valve/gfx/env folder.
