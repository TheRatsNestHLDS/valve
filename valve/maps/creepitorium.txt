INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip creepitorium.zip.

2. 	Open the creepitorium folder and place the creepitorium.bsp, 				    			creepitorium.res, and creepitorium.txt in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	Place the 6 (six) grave256_tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Place the sound otic folder in your valve/sound folder.

5.	Place the model folder (psycho) in your valve/models folder.
